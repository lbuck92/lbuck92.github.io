curriculum vitae

Hello! I'm Lauren. I'm in my final year of my Computer Science Ph.D. at Vanderbilt University. I work in the Learning in Virtual Environments (LIVE) Lab developing 3D virtual environments and determining the quality of virtual reality (VR) through the evaluation of human behavior.

research interests

My research lies at the intersection of computer science, cognitive psychology and neuroscience.
