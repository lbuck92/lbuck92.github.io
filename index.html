Hello there! It looks like you've found my little corner of the internet.
This website is currently under construction, so please bear with me.

I currently work at Vanderbilt University in the Learning in Virtual Environments (LIVE) lab developing 3D virtual environments and determining the fidelity of virtual reality through the evaluation of human behavior in these environments. I do lots of programming and psychological evaluation, in short. Some of the projects I've done, am currently working on, or are in the still-an-idea baby development stages include:

  • Spatial Perception in VR
  • Collaborative Affordances and Social Proxemics in VR
  • 3D Bodily Maps of Emotions in VR
  • Peripersonal Space in VR
  • Applications for Wayfinding and Navigation in Augmented Reality

If you are interested in virtual & augmented reality and the projects listed here, please check back at a later date to see project descriptions and links to papers published concerning this work. :)
