Hello there! My name is Lauren and I'm a 4th year PhD student at Vanderbilt University. I work in the Learning in Virtual Environments (LIVE) lab developing 3D virtual environments and determining the fidelity of virtual reality through the evaluation of human behavior in these environments. I do lots of programming and psychological evaluation, in short.

Some of the projects I've done, am currently working on, or are in the still-an-idea baby development stages include:

  • Collaborative Navigation and Spatial Knowledge Acquisition in Immersive Virtual Environments (IVEs)
  • Collaborative Affordances and Social Proxemics in IVEs
  • Peripersonal Space Perception in IVEs
  • 3D Bodily Maps of Emotions using IVEs
  • Spatial Perception in IVEs
